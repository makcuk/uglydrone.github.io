About

UglyDrone is a rugged, modular platform for building drones. It is designed to be easy to use, easy to modify, and easy to repair.

It is modular, highly field-configurable, and can be used for a variety of applications—from a simple FPV drone with a loitering payload to an autonomous aerial vehicle with complex missions utilizing AI and computer vision.

Drones can be stacked together to form a multi-drone system, which can be deployed as a containerized unit from various platforms: aerial, land, or sea.

With modern mesh radio technology from TenetaLabs®, a drone constellation can operate autonomously, splitting missions and providing intelligence to each other.
